About Our Company

We are the fastest growing chain of family fish right out of Kentucky. We started our business in 1982 under the sycamore tree in Pop Rainer's backyard.The fish pond was full of beautiful Rainbow fish, developed by Pop Rainer himself. Our neighbors, friends, and communitycame to see the spectactular fish and we were featured on the local news channel.
